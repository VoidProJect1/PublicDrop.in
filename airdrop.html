Frequently Asked Questions
Is it really free? Are there hidden fees? +

Yes — completely free. PublicDrop never charges participation fees, gas fees for registration, or any hidden costs. Token projects fund these airdrops as a marketing initiative. You only pay standard network gas fees if you choose to move your tokens after receiving them.

Is my wallet safe? Do you need my private key? +

Your private key is never requested or stored. We only use your public wallet address for airdrop distribution. When you connect via MetaMask, you only sign a read-only request — no funds can be moved without your explicit confirmation in your wallet app.

When will I receive my tokens? +

For live campaigns, tokens are distributed within 24–72 hours after the campaign end date. For upcoming campaigns, tokens are sent within 24–72 hours of the campaign launch and your eligibility confirmation. You'll receive tokens directly to your connected wallet address.

Which wallets are supported? +

We support all major EVM-compatible wallets (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, Rainbow, etc.) as well as Solana wallets (Phantom, Solflare). You can also manually enter any valid EVM (0x...) or Solana address without connecting a wallet.

Do I need to complete all tasks to claim? +

Not all tasks are mandatory — completing more tasks increases your allocation multiplier and point score. However, some campaigns may require a minimum number of completed tasks to qualify for the base allocation. Check each campaign's requirements carefully.
