🎯 Our Mission

At PublicDrop.in, we believe every Indian should have access to the same quality of crypto market data that institutional traders use — completely free.

We built PublicDrop because most crypto tools are either too technical, too expensive, or designed for Western markets. Indian investors deserve a platform that speaks their language, supports INR, and is built with their regulatory environment in mind.

What We Offer

  • Live prices for 500+ cryptocurrencies powered by Binance API
  • DexScreener integration for meme and emerging tokens
  • Portfolio tracker with real-time P&L and allocation charts
  • Verified airdrop campaigns with wallet-based claims
  • Crypto news aggregation from 50+ global sources
  • Complete beginner education in simple language

Our Technology

We use the Binance API for exchange-traded coin data (no rate limits, 30-second refresh), DexScreener for DeFi and meme token discovery, and CoinCap for coin images. All data is fetched directly in your browser — we don't store or sell your data.

Our Promise

PublicDrop will always be free for individual users. We don't show pop-up ads, we don't sell user data, and we don't require registration to access market data.

India Regulatory Environment

Cryptocurrency trading in India is legal but regulated. Key regulations include:

  • TDS: 1% TDS on crypto transactions above ₹50,000 (or ₹10,000 for specified persons)
  • Income Tax: 30% flat tax on crypto gains + 4% cess from FY 2022-23
  • PMLA: Crypto exchanges must comply with Prevention of Money Laundering Act
  • VDA: Crypto classified as Virtual Digital Assets under Finance Act 2022

Always consult a Chartered Accountant for personalized tax advice.
